Phased Array

Unit Cell

The designed unit cell is an antipodal Vivaldi antenna operating over a wide frequency band from 1 to 10 GHz. It is optimized for broadband performance with an S11 response below −10 dB and stable impedance characteristics.

Top View
Bottom View

Array

After optimizing the unit cell, it is replicated and arranged to form array structures according to application requirements. Arrays of 1×2, 1×4, and 1×8 elements are designed to enhance gain and directivity while maintaining performance across the desired frequency band.
